The most recent version of MakerBot® MakerWare™, the software that drives your MakerBot®  Desktop 3D Printer, is now available for download.Version 2.4.1 has some big upgrades, particularly for MakerBot® Dissolvable Filament for MakerBot® Replicator® 2X Experimental 3D Printers.The newest version of MakerWare comes equipped with a new custom profile for MakerBot Dissolvable Filament.
