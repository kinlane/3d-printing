Can 3D printing go green?Bay area conference to brainstorm on ethics & sustainability of 3D printing April 29 With 3D printing gaining traction as the democratization of manufacturing, now is the time to discuss the environmental impact and envision a sustainable future for this rapidly growing industry.To anser the question ‘Can 3D printing go green?’ Swissnex, an organization that fosters connections between Switzerland and San Francisco, is coordinating a multi-day forum on 3D printing and biomaterials.All are welcome to attend The Ethics and Sustainability of 3D Printing conference this Thursday in San Francisco.
